Why Space ETFs Right Now

A new wave of launches, IPOs, and government contracts is reshaping the sector. Here's what's driving the interest.

🚀

IPO Wave

A growing list of space-economy companies — from launch providers to satellite operators — are heading toward public markets, expanding the universe of investable names.

🛰️

Diversified Exposure

Space ETFs spread risk across launch, satellite, communications, and defense-adjacent companies — instead of betting on a single rocket company.

🏛️

Government Tailwinds

Defense and civil space budgets continue to expand globally, supporting long-term contracts for many of the holdings inside these funds.

📡

Satellite Internet Boom

Low-earth-orbit broadband is scaling fast, driving revenue for satellite manufacturers, launch providers, and ground infrastructure companies.
